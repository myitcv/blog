    • FAQ

    Can I do the course remotely?

    No - the logistics of doing this over a webcast or similar are going to be too much...

    Who is going to be running the sessions?

    Me - hence the limit to the size of class

    Is there a 'taster' session?

    Yes - the first session will be open to all (subject to the capacity of the lecture theatre). Sign-up will then be on a first come, first served basis for session 2-6

    Can I take this course for credit?

    No - this sites entirely outside the LBS course portfolio

    Can I miss a class?

    Short answer is no, simply because you will immediately lose pace with the rest of the class



    Long answer is, possibly, if you are willing to attend tutorial sessions and put in the effort to catch up

    How is this similar to courses offered by General Assembly?

    This course will be very similar to any course that purports to be an "Introduction to Ruby on Rails." This includes any GA course by that title, Coursera online courses, books etc. free or otherwise


    The main difference to online courses is that this will be a fully interactive "in-person" session
